Jason Luttmann

Game developer, sound designer, and musician behind depressive horror titles and various skramz projects. Jason is 23 years old and has been obsessed with horror games since he was 13, when he first played Amnesia: The Dark Descent at a friend's house. Further taken by games such as Penumbra: Overture, Fingerbones, and Cry of Fear, he grew up taking a particular interest in dark and disturbing media.

Jason graduated from Ohio University with a Bachelor's Degree in music production in 2023. Present day, Jason hopes to contribute to the same sphere of blackened content that has helped define his whole life.
